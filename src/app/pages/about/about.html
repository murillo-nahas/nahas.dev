About me

Hey! I'm Murillo Nahas, a full-stack software engineer with a strong focus on building scalable, real-world systems. Most of my work lives in the JavaScript/TypeScript ecosystem, where I build Angular frontends and NestJS backends inside large, modular monorepos. I enjoy understanding how things work end to end, from UI state management and performance to APIs, databases, and infrastructure, and I'm naturally drawn to problems that require both technical depth and clear reasoning.

At Merch, I design and ship features used in production by real teams, often touching multiple layers of system. I work with Angular, Node.js, NestJS, MongoDB, Prisma, and AWS-based services, and I care deeply about code quality, architecture, and long-term maintainability. Beyond writing code, I spend a lot of time thinking about system design, modularity, developer experience, and how to evolve large, existing systems without breaking what already works.

What motivates me most is impact and growth. I like solving complex problems, mentoring through discussions and code reviews, and constantly refining how I think about software engineering. I'm actively pushing toward a senior-level mindset, with the goal of working on high-scale products and eventually contributing at a global level. Outside of coding, I value discipline, consistency, and balance, traits I bring into both my technical work and personal life.
